I am an incoming IT Graduate at RWE focusing on Data Science. I enjoy combining engineering first principles with emerging digital technologies to tackle challenging problems. My career interests lie at the intersection of digitalization of Renewable Energy Systems, AI and business innovations.

Previously, I interned in the IoT departments at BMW Group (Munich DE in 2023) implementing end-to-end data integration strategy for EV battery cell production, LORIA (Nancy FR in 2022) co-simulating the subsystems of vehicles powered by fuel cells & supercapacitors and Industrial Technology Research Institute ITRI (Taipei TW in 2019) conducting user market research.

I graduated with a double master's in Decentralized Smart Energy Systems from KTH Royal Institute of Technology & Université de Lorraine (2023) as an Erasmus Mundus Scholar. I received my undergraduate degree at National Taiwan University (2021), with a major in mechanical engineering as a MOFA Scholar.

When I’m not coding or prompting, I like to compete in football leagues, travel the world with friends and read about global socio-economic & political issues.
